Our venue, is [Platform 9RM](https://platf9rm.com/location/hove-town-hall/) which is a lovely co-working space, on the ground Floor in Hove Town Hall, The entrance is by the eastbound bus stop on Church Street. The address is Hove Town Hall, Church Road, BN3 2AF

---

### *Arriving by train...*

Platform 9RM is about a 10 minute walk from Hove Train Station

---

### *Arriving by car...*

Parking is available in the multi-storey [car park](https://en.parkopedia.co.uk/parking/carpark/norton_road/bn3/brighton_and_hove/?arriving=202210010900&leaving=202210011100) opposite Hove Town Hall.
